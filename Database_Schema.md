# Consolidated Firestore Database Schema (Multi-Tenant)

This schema utilizes Firestore's document-model hierarchy. All school-specific operational data is stored in sub-collections beneath the parent `schools` document to ensure data isolation.

---

## 1. Global Platform Collections (Root Level)

### `system_admins`
Platform-level users who manage school subscriptions and tenant onboarding.
- `admin_id` (String, PK)
- `email` (String)
- `access_level` (Enum: SUPER_ADMIN, SUPPORT)
- `created_at` (Timestamp)

### `schools`
The core tenant document. Every client school gets one document here.
- `school_id` (String, PK - e.g., 'burlington_central_01')
- `school_name` (String)
- `subscription_tier` (Enum: BASIC, PRO, ENTERPRISE)
- `status` (Enum: ACTIVE, SUSPENDED)
- `settings` (Map)
  - `periods_per_day` (Number)
  - `period_duration_mins` (Number)
  - `allow_email_notifications` (Boolean)

---

## 2. Tenant-Scoped Collections (Sub-Collections)
*Path format: `schools/{school_id}/{collection_name}/{document_id}`*

### `users`
Handles authentication, profiles, and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for the specific school.
- `uid` (String, PK - Matches Firebase Auth UID)
- `role` (Enum: PRINCIPAL, COORDINATOR, TEACHER, STUDENT)
- `first_name`, `last_name` (String)
- `email` (String)
- `is_active` (Boolean)

### `classrooms`
Physical resources available for scheduling.
- `room_id` (String, PK)
- `code` (String) - e.g. "RM101"
- `name` (String - e.g., "Main Gym")
- `capacity` (Number)
- `facility_type` (Enum: REGULAR, LAB, GYM)
- `is_gym` (Boolean - Legacy flag)

### `subjects`
The academic catalog offered by the school.
- `subject_id` (String, PK)
- `code` (String) - e.g. "MAT10"
- `name` (String)
- `grade_level` (Number)
- `required_periods_per_week` (Number)
- `facility_type` (Enum: REGULAR, LAB, GYM)

### `rules`
The JSON-based logic engine for subject enrollment prerequisites.
- `rule_id` (String, PK)
- `target_subject_id` (Reference to `subjects`)
- `logic_tree` (Map/JSON)
  - *Example:* `{ "condition": "AND", "rules": [{"prerequisite": "Physics 9", "operator": ">=", "value": 70}] }`

### `teachers`
Staff availability and constraints for the optimization engine.
- `teacher_id` (String, PK)
- `user_id` (Reference to `users`)
- `specializations` (Array of Strings) - Maps to Subject Codes or Names
- `max_periods_per_week` (Number)
- `off_times` (Array of Numbers) - List of period indices (1-indexed) where busy

### `students`
Student academic profiles, requested courses, and notification tracking.
- `student_id` (String, PK)
- `user_id` (Reference to `users`)
- `grade_level` (Number)
- `historical_grades` (Map: `{ "subject_id": percentage_score }`)
- `requested_subjects` (Array of `subject_id`s)
- `last_schedule_email_status` (Enum: PENDING, DELIVERED, BOUNCED)

#### `student_schedules` (Sub-collection)
*Path: `schools/{s}/students/{st}/student_schedules/{sched}`*
- `schedule_id` (Reference)
- `semester` (Number)
- `assignments` (Array of Maps) - Personalized timetable entries
- `updated_at` (Timestamp)

### `schedules`
The finalized output generated by the OR-Tools algorithm.
- `schedule_id` (String, PK)
- `semester` (Number: 1 or 2)
- `status` (Enum: DRAFT, PUBLISHED)
- `published_at` (Timestamp)
- `conflict_report` (Map) - Detailed results from the solver
- `assignments` (Array of Maps)
  - `period_name` (String)
  - `subject_id` (Reference)
  - `teacher_id` (Reference)
  - `room_id` (Reference)
  - `enrolled_student_ids` (Array of Strings)

### `attendance`
Daily tracking linked to specific schedule assignments.
- `attendance_id` (String, PK)
- `schedule_id` (Reference to `schedules`)
- `date` (Timestamp)
- `period_name` (String)
- `absent_student_ids` (Array of Strings)
